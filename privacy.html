Privacy Policy

How we handle information on this website.

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Ruscal Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the ruscaltech.com website (the "Site"). We take privacy seriously and limit data collection to what is necessary for understanding how the Site is used.

Information We Collect

We do not operate any contact forms, user accounts, or comment systems. We do not deploy advertising networks or behavioral tracking on this Site.

We do use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data about how visitors interact with the Site. This data helps us understand which pages are visited and how the Site is navigated, but it does not identify you personally.

Google Analytics

This Site uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Referring website or search term

This data is collected using cookies and similar technologies and is transmitted to and stored by Google. Google may use this data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We have configured Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled, meaning your full IP address is not stored.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by using a browser with tracking protection enabled. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Information Collected by Our Hosting Provider

This Site is hosted on GitHub Pages, a static site hosting service operated by GitHub, Inc. When you visit the Site, GitHub's servers may automatically collect standard server log data, which may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • The pages you visit and the date and time of your visit
  • The referring URL (if any)

This data is collected and processed by GitHub in accordance with their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review GitHub's Privacy Statement for more information.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies on your device to distinguish unique visitors and track sessions. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information. GitHub Pages may also set functional cookies as part of its hosting infrastructure. We do not set any first-party cookies beyond those placed by these services.

Third-Party Links

The Site contains links to external websites, including our GitHub organization (github.com/Ruscal-Technologies). These external sites are governed by their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data practices.

Data Retention

We do not directly store personal data. Analytics data collected via Google Analytics is retained in accordance with Google's data retention settings and policies. Server log data collected by GitHub Pages is subject to their retention policies.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
